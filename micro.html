    Orthologs

    To identify orthologous groups among the four sequenced genomes(Cucumis sativus L., C.sativus var.h ardwickii, Cucumis melo L., Citrullus lanatus), OrthoMCL (Li, Stoeckert et al. 2003) was used.

    First, all-against-all BLASTP compare the set of protein sequences from the eleven speices with an E-value cutoff of 1e-5. Then, the result of BLASTP was analyzed by OrthoMCL algorithm. Last, orthologous groups were clustered by MCL (Enright, Van Dongen et al. 2002) (I=1.5) algorithm.

    The software versions were: OrthoMCL version 2.05, MCL version mcl 12-068, and NCBI BLAST version 2.2.15.
